About Me

Hello! Here is a little about me. I graduated May 2018 with a Digital Media Production degree and an emphasis in Live Studio and Remote Production, and also a minor in Sports Communication. I have experience as video producer, editor, director, videographer, and content creator. I have worked at KMOS-TV and have had internships with Nine Network, USSSA Midwest Network, and Zimmerman Productions during my four years at the University of Central Missouri. After graduating college I worked in the live events industry for about 2 years. Currently, I live in Kansas City and am learning to become a Full Stack Developer through the University of Kansas and am looking forward to jump starting my career in the Coding field.
